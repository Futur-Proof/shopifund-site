Plain-language summary: shopifund is a matching marketplace, not a lender. We collect your business information to connect you with funding partners. We do not sell your personal information. We share your data only with lenders you are matched with and the service providers that help us operate.

Section 01

Who We Are

shopifund ("shopifund," "we," "our," or "us") operates shopifund.com and any related applications, tools, or services we provide (collectively, the "Services"). We are a business funding marketplace that connects small and medium-sized businesses ("you" or "your business") with third-party lending partners.

shopifund is not a lender, bank, credit union, or financial institution. We do not make credit decisions and do not fund loans. All lending and credit decisions are made by independent third-party lending partners in our network.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our Services, how we use and share that information, and the choices you have regarding your information. By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Section 02

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

When you use our application wizard, contact us, or otherwise interact with our Services, you may provide:

  • Business information: business name, entity type, industry, ZIP code, date of business formation, and monthly revenue
  • Funding request details: requested loan amount, funding purpose, and desired timeline
  • Personal contact information: your name, email address, and phone number
  • Financial indicators: estimated credit score range and business bank account information (only where you elect to provide it through our optional bank connection feature)
  • Communications: any messages, inquiries, or feedback you send to us

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit shopifund.com, our servers and third-party analytics partners automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (city/state level)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, time spent on each page, and click paths
  • Referring URLs and search terms that led you to our site
  • Form interaction data (which steps were completed, where users drop off)

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties in limited circumstances, such as:

  • Analytics and advertising partners who provide aggregated audience data
  • Publicly available business directories or databases used to verify business information
  • Referring partners or affiliates who directed you to shopifund

We do not purchase lists of personal data from data brokers or third-party consumer databases. We do not conduct hard credit inquiries. Any soft inquiry or credit-range estimation is based solely on information you self-report.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Purpose Information Used
Match you with lenders — identify lending partners whose criteria match your business profile and funding needs Business info, revenue, credit range, funding amount, timeline, entity type, industry
Deliver your matches — send match results, lender introductions, and follow-up communications Name, email address, phone number
Improve our Services — analyze form completion patterns, identify drop-off points, and improve the matching experience Form interaction data, analytics data
Communicate with you — respond to your inquiries, send service-related notices, and provide updates on your match status Name, email address, phone number
Marketing — send promotional content about our Services or lending products, with your consent where required by law Email address, funding interests
Fraud prevention and security — detect and prevent fraudulent submissions or misuse of our Services IP address, device data, form interaction patterns
Legal compliance — comply with applicable laws, regulations, and lawful requests from authorities Any information required by law

We do not use your information to make automated credit decisions. All credit evaluations are performed by our independent lending partners under their own privacy policies and underwriting standards.

Section 04

How We Share Your Information

shopifund shares your information in the following ways. We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Lending Partners

When you complete our application and request matches, we share your business and contact information with the lending partners we match you with. Each lending partner operates under its own privacy policy. Once shared, your information is subject to that lender's terms. We encourage you to review each lender's privacy policy before proceeding with an application.

Service Providers

We work with third-party vendors who help us operate our platform, including:

  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Email delivery and CRM platforms
  • Analytics and product analytics tools (e.g., session recording, funnel analysis)
  • Customer support software
  • Bank account verification providers (where used)

These providers access your information only to perform services on our behalf and are contractually prohibited from using it for other purposes.

Referring Partners and Affiliates

If you arrived at shopifund through a referring partner or affiliate, we may share aggregated, non-identifying performance data (such as whether a referral converted) with that partner. We do not share your personal information with referring partners unless you explicitly consent.

Legal Requirements and Business Transfers

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, or valid legal process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of shopifund, our users, or the public. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our business, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

We do not sell your personal information. Sharing your data with matched lenders for the purpose of fulfilling your loan inquiry is not a "sale" under applicable privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act.

Section 05

Your Privacy Choices

Marketing Communications

If you receive promotional emails from us, you may opt out at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email or by contacting us at privacy@shopifund.com. Please allow up to 10 business days for your request to take effect. Note that even after opting out of marketing, you may still receive transactional emails related to your loan inquiry or active matches.

Access and Correction

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct inaccuracies, by contacting us at privacy@shopifund.com. We will respond within 30 days. Note that certain information may be retained as required by law or legitimate business purposes.

Deletion

You may request that we delete the personal information we hold about you. We will honor such requests to the extent permitted by law. Certain records may need to be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution purposes.

Do Not Track

Some browsers transmit "Do Not Track" signals to websites. Our site does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals. You may use browser settings or third-party tools to limit tracking technologies on our site.

Section 06

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate our Services, remember your preferences, and analyze usage patterns. The types of cookies we use include:

Essential Cookies

Required for our site to function. These enable session state, form progress retention, and security. They cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.

Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how visitors use our site — which pages they visit, how long they stay, and where they exit. This data is aggregated and used to improve our Services. We use tools such as Google Analytics for this purpose.

Marketing and Retargeting Cookies

Used to show you relevant advertisements on other platforms after you visit shopifund.com. These are set by third-party advertising platforms. You can opt out of interest-based advertising through the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool or your browser's cookie settings.

You may configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when cookies are being set. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features of our Services.

Section 07

Information Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security assessments of our infrastructure and third-party vendors

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we work hard to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@shopifund.com.

Section 08

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Active inquiries: retained for the duration of your match window and any follow-on lending process
  • Completed or closed inquiries: retained for up to 3 years for fraud prevention, legal compliance, and service improvement
  • Marketing data: retained until you opt out, after which we retain only a suppression record to honor your preference
  • Analytics data: retained in aggregated, non-identifying form indefinitely

When your information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Section 09

Third-Party Services

Our Services contain links to third-party websites, and our matching process connects you to third-party lenders. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected and processed by shopifund. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of:

  • Lending partners and financial institutions you are matched with
  • Websites you navigate to from links on our platform
  • Referring or affiliate partners

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third party before submitting your personal information. Each lender in our network operates independently and is responsible for its own data practices.

Section 10

Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended solely for use by businesses and their authorized representatives who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, we will promptly delete it. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us at privacy@shopifund.com.

Section 11

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) grant you specific rights regarding your personal information.

Your California Rights

  • Right to Know: You may request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, our business purposes, and the third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing: shopifund does not sell your personal information. We share data with matched lenders as a core function of our service, which is not considered a "sale" under CCPA.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: Where we process sensitive personal information (such as financial account data used in bank verification), you may request that we limit its use to the purposes disclosed in this policy.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To submit a California privacy rights request, contact us at privacy@shopifund.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will respond within 45 days. You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf; we may require verification of the agent's authority.

California residents who have questions about our data practices or wish to exercise any of the rights described above may also contact us by mail at the address listed in Section 13.

When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you by email or by displaying a notice on our website.

Your continued use of our Services after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
